## Brand Overview

### What is iRaceHub?

iRaceHub is an **open-source league management platform** designed specifically for the iRacing community. It empowers league owners to easily manage leagues, series, and seasons—while providing drivers with a modern, integrated experience for viewing schedules, results, standings, and competing in league-wide events.

### Key Features Drivers Should Know About

- **Real-time Schedule Sync** - Automatically syncs schedules with iRacing
- **Instant Results** - Race results are imported and displayed instantly
- **Live Standings** - Always see where you stand in real-time
- **Virtual Economy** - Participate in league-wide virtual money systems
- **Accessible Dashboards** - Simple, intuitive interface for drivers
- **Community-Driven** - Open source and built by the community, for the community

### Why Drivers Should Promote iRaceHub

- **Enhances League Experience** - Makes league management transparent and efficient
- **Open Source** - No vendor lock-in; free for all leagues
- **Community First** - Built to serve drivers, not extract value
- **Reliable** - Battle-tested in production leagues
- **Supporting Innovation** - Help shape the future of league management

## Logo Usage Guidelines

### Primary Logo

The iRaceHub logo is the primary brand mark and should be used in most applications.

**Usage:**

- ✅ Use on white, black, and neutral backgrounds
- ✅ Maintain clear space around the logo (minimum 10% of logo width)
- ✅ Never rotate or skew the logo
- ✅ Never add effects, shadows, or gradients
- ✅ Ensure sufficient contrast with background

**Don't:**

- ❌ Change colors without approval
- ❌ Stretch or distort the logo
- ❌ Place on busy backgrounds that reduce visibility
- ❌ Use outdated versions
- ❌ Recreate the logo from scratch

### Logo Variations

- **Full Logo** - Logo with "iRaceHub" wordmark (preferred)
- **Icon Only** - Mark without text (for social media profiles, favicons)
- **Horizontal Layout** - Logo and text side-by-side
- **Vertical Layout** - Logo and text stacked
- **Monochrome** - Single color version for limited-color applications

### Minimum Size

- **Digital:** 200px width minimum
- **Print:** 1 inch width minimum

### Clear Space

Maintain a clear space equal to 10% of the logo's width on all sides.

## Branding Guidelines

### Do's ✅

- **Do** maintain professional tone in all communications
- **Do** use the official logo in its original form
- **Do** emphasize community and open-source aspects
- **Do** highlight specific features most relevant to your audience
- **Do** maintain consistent branding across all platforms
- **Do** credit iRaceHub when appropriate
- **Do** use current logos and brand assets (not outdated versions)
- **Do** ensure accessibility (contrast, alt text, etc.)

### Don'ts ❌

- **Don't** alter or recreate the logo
- **Don't** use misleading claims about features
- **Don't** misrepresent iRaceHub as closed-source or paid
- **Don't** violate iRacing's own branding guidelines
- **Don't** use poor-quality or low-resolution assets
- **Don't** combine iRaceHub branding inappropriately with competitors
- **Don't** imply official endorsement without approval

## How to Promote in iRacing

### In-Game Opportunities

1. **Driver Center Profile**
   - Add iRaceHub league info to your profile
   - Example: "Competing in [League Name], managed with iRaceHub"

2. **League Decals/Liveries**
   - Create custom liveries featuring the iRaceHub logo
   - Use your league URL as part of the design
   - Share livery designs in community forums

3. **Team Names & Paint Schemes**
   - Incorporate "iRaceHub" into team names
   - Feature the branding in your paint schemes

### Community Forum Promotions

1. **League Announcement Posts**
   - Mention iRaceHub when advertising your league
   - Share tangible benefits drivers experience
   - Include links to standings or results pages

2. **Forum Signature**
   - Add a brief iRaceHub mention to your forum signature
   - Example: "Running my league with iRaceHub - free, open-source league management"

3. **Helpful Responses**
   - When drivers ask about league management tools, recommend iRaceHub
   - Provide genuine experiences and comparisons
   - Be authentic and helpful, not spammy

### VOD/Stream Promotion

1. **Overlay Graphics**
   - Include iRaceHub branding in stream overlays during league races
   - Display live standings from iRaceHub

2. **Race Commentary**
   - Mention league features powered by iRaceHub during streams
   - Highlight standings, payouts, or schedule information
   - Share stories about how iRaceHub improved your league

3. **Stream Title/Description**
   ```
   iRacing League Race | Powered by iRaceHub | [Race Name]
   ```

### External Website Promotion

1. **League Website**
   - Embed standings widget from iRaceHub
   - Link to iRaceHub GitHub repo
   - Add iRaceHub branding to footer

2. **League Announcements**
   - Feature iRaceHub in league newsletters
   - Share tips for using iRaceHub features
   - Highlight community feedback incorporated into updates

3. **Blog/Articles**
   - Write case studies about using iRaceHub
   - Share how specific features improved your league
   - Compare before/after experiences

## Best Practices Summary

### ✅ Do These Things

| Practice                                | Why                                       |
| --------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Share genuine experiences               | Authenticity resonates with the community |
| Use official logos correctly            | Maintains professional appearance         |
| Highlight community/open-source aspects | This is iRaceHub's biggest selling point  |
| Provide real links to resources         | Helps drivers get started                 |
| Credit iRaceHub appropriately           | Shows respect for the project             |
| Keep branding consistent                | Builds recognition and trust              |

### ❌ Avoid These Things

| Problem                     | Impact                               |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Misleading claims           | Damages credibility and trust        |
| Poor quality assets         | Reflects poorly on the project       |
| Spammy promotion            | Gets ignored, hurts the community    |
| Implied endorsements        | Creates confusion and liability      |
| Outdated information        | Makes drivers think project is stale |
| Ignoring iRacing guidelines | Can result in content removal        |
